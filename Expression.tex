№9(1) Вычислить:	\[\left((\left(2.000000\right)^{(\left(2.000000\right)*x)})\right)'


Решение:
	\[\left(x\right)'
	= \left(1.000000\right)\]
	\[\left(\left(2.000000\right)\right)'
	= \left(0.000000\right)\]
	\[\left((\left(2.000000\right)*x)\right)'
	= ((\left(0.000000\right)*x)+(\left(2.000000\right)*\left(1.000000\right)))\]
	\[\left(ln{\left(2.000000\right)}\right)'
	= \left(0.000000\right)\]
	\[\left((ln{\left(2.000000\right)}*(\left(2.000000\right)*x))\right)'
	= ((\left(0.000000\right)*(\left(2.000000\right)*x))+(ln{\left(2.000000\right)}*((\left(0.000000\right)*x)+(\left(2.000000\right)*\left(1.000000\right)))))\]
	\[\left((\left(2.000000\right)^{(\left(2.000000\right)*x)})\right)'
	= ((\left(2.000000\right)^{(\left(2.000000\right)*x)})*((\left(0.000000\right)*(\left(2.000000\right)*x))+(ln{\left(2.000000\right)}*((\left(0.000000\right)*x)+(\left(2.000000\right)*\left(1.000000\right))))))\]
	\[\left((\left(2.000000\right)^{(\left(2.000000\right)*x)})\right)'
	= ((\left(2.000000\right)^{(\left(2.000000\right)*x)})*\left(1.386294\right))\]
